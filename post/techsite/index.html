    Techsite

    Thu, Apr 9, 2020 2-minute read 49/100

    Naturally gain popularity in the tech community. Techsite.io is a free marketplace for publishing and discovering tech stories, software and startups.

    Most of you are using technology of some kind to build you business or to even change the world!

    At Techsite.io we have recently launched techsite.io where you can follow the lates technology and start-up news from US, UK, China, Finland and Sweden. And don’t worry, you don’t need to know Chinese or even Swedish, as all news is automatically translated to English.

    As a selected entrepreneur, and therefore important influencer, we would love if you could also sign up to the service for free at techsite.io/signup. You can register in a simple way using Facebook login. Articles that you ”Like” will be posted on the first page and your image will show on that article. You can also make comments, post articles and collect articles by only providing the link to the article you like anywhere from the web. Additionally you will get you own page where you can collect and share interesting articles.

    You will find the selected articles on the first page, and all articles from each country using the hamburger menu on the top. You can also search for specific topics like Bitcoin, Python, Spotify or even Trump and find a lot of interesting news from the last six months!

    A very convenient feature is that you can share articles to your social media accounts directly from the front page or when reading the article.

    You can share to Twitter, LinkedIn, Facebook, e-mail, etc.
